Introduction

I am Assistant Professor at Lee Kong Chian School of Medicine, Nanyang Technological University in Singapore. My research employs data analytics and mathematical modelling to understand the transmission and control of infectious pathogens. I have a particular interest in vector-borne and respiratory diseases, as well as spatio-temporal epidemiology. Key research projects for my laboratory now include the use of high-resolution, high-dimensional databases to understand:

(1) the impact of novel vector control tools on vector-borne disease transmission

(2) the forecastability of disease burden over large spatial scales

(3) long-term post-acute consequences and complications of patients infected by vector-borne and respiratory diseases and

(4) environmental risk factors for vector-borne and respiratory disease transmission
