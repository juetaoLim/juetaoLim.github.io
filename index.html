Introduction

I am Assistant Professor at Lee Kong Chian School of Medicine, Nanyang Technological University in Singapore. My research employs data analytics and mathematical modelling to understand the transmission and control of infectious pathogens. I have a particular interest in arboviruses and respiratory pathogens, as well as spatio-temporal epidemiology. Key research projects for my laboratory now include the use of high-resolution, high-dimensional databases to understand:

(1) the impact of novel vector control tools on arboviral transmission

(2) the forecastability of disease burden over large spatial scales and

(3) long-term post-acute consequences and complications of arboviral and respiratory infection
